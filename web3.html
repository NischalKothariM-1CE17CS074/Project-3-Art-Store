LORD GAUTHAMA BUDDHA

by BHATTACHARYA

The portrait of LORD GAUTHAMA BUDDHA was painted in 1606 by the Indian artist BHATTACHARYA, and today hangs in the Indonessia. It is the third of three portraits of the World that the family painted that year.

Date: 1606
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Indonessia
